The Problem

The main problem we are trying to solve with our app is the lack of knowledge about Swine Flu in Jalisco, to make this possible we are going to give information about how to prevent it, how to know if you are sick, what to do if you are sick, if you feel bad or both. Jalisco has had many cases these past months. Over six hundred confirmed cases of Swine Flu of different ages. The worst mistake people had done is to confuse the Swine Flu with the common cold. Treating on with the other can end in death in either case.

Our Solution

We will try to give a solution to this problem creating an application that will share the knowledge of how to detect the Swine Flue, what to do if you or someone you know has it, where to go if medical attention is needed, and many more aspects about this disease that everyone must be aware of. This application will be simple and very usefull to everyone of any age and it will prepare the user to confront the Swine Flu.
